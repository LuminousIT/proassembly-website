Who we are

The Lord gave us a vision to bring people of different tribes, race, cultures, churches, countries and nations of the world, like minded believers that are set to bring the kingdom of God to bear on earth with the name PROPHETIC ASSEMBLY on Nov 15th, 2012. The first edition of this gathering came up first weekend in January 2013.

Our Vision

Prophetic Assembly is aimed at raising, empowering and releasing end time army for kingdom growth. Breaking the yoke of the devil and setting the oppressed free in Christ name.
